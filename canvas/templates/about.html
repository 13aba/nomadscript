About us | Nomad Script

Our Mission

At Nomad Script, we are dedicated to preserving and revitalizing the traditional Mongolian script through interactive learning and modern technology. Our platform serves as both an educational tool for beginners and a collaborative space for fluent users to contribute to a growing dataset of handwritten Mongolian letters.

Our Vision

Our near term plan is to increase the number of supported languages to reach even wider audience, and add interactive events such as leaderboard for contributors and more engaging video lessons for the beginners. Our broader vision is to remain an open-source project while expanding with additional tools, such as an API for direct database access.
